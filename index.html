PART 2 · PLATFORM RISK

Three Risks the Platform Cannot Ignore

从卖家视角观察到的三个平台侧风险

01Ad Revenue at Risk  广告收入受冲击
When a buyer orders via Agent, the search results page is bypassed entirely — zero ad impressions. Amazon earns ~$47B/yr from ads. This is a structural threat.  /  买家通过 Agent 下单，搜索页被完全绕过，广告曝光归零。亚马逊每年约 470 亿美元广告收入面临结构性威胁。
02Behavioral Data Bypassed  用户意图数据旁路
Amazon's moat is behavioral data — what users search, browse, compare. Agent-mediated purchases bypass this entirely, starving Rufus of training signals.  /  亚马逊的护城河是行为数据。Agent 中介购买完全绕过这一环节，使 Rufus 失去训练信号。
03Customer Experience Escapes  客户体验失控
Reviews, returns, repeat-purchase nudges — Amazon's entire post-purchase loyalty loop disappears when the relationship lives in a third-party chat app.  /  评论、退货、复购引导——当关系存在于第三方 App 时，亚马逊所有售后忠诚机制都消失了。

PART 3 · LISTING RETHINK

From Keyword Container to Knowledge System

从「关键词容器」到「结构化产品知识系统」— COSMO 改变了 Rufus 识别产品的方式

❌ Old Logic  ·  Traditional Amazon SEO
Search: Keyword exact match  关键词精确匹配
"waterproof hiking backpack 60L"
Ranking: Keyword density + sales + reviews  关键词密度 + 销量 + 评分
Content: Pack in as many keywords as possible  尽量多塞关键词
Trust signal: Review count + star rating  评论数量 + 星级
✅ Rufus GEO  ·  New Logic
Search: Natural language conversation  自然语言对话
"What do I need for a weekend camping trip?"
Recall: 15 COSMO relation coverage + intent match  15种关系覆盖 + 意图匹配
Content: Answer real buyer questions, scene-first  回答真实问题，场景优先
Trust signal: UGC = Ground Truth  Q&A + 评论权重高于品牌自述
Core Principle: Every sentence in a Listing should be recognizable by COSMO as evidence for a specific relation type — not just a keyword signal.  Listing 写的每一句话，都应该能被 COSMO 识别为某种关系类型的证据。
PART 3 · COSMO FRAMEWORK

COSMO: The 15-Relation Framework Behind Rufus

Rufus 底层的结构化关系 — 我们用它审计每一个 Listing，确保 AI 能全面理解产品

COSMO defines 15 (head, relation, tail) triples for RAG-based product recall. We test every Listing against all 15 before going live. Passing score: ≥10/15 ✅

USED_FOR_FUNC
What is it mainly used for?  主要功能是什么？
USED_FOR_EVE  ⚠️
What occasion / activity?  适合什么场合？（最易漏）
USED_FOR_AUD
Who is it designed for?  为谁设计？
CAPABLE_OF
What special capability?  有什么特殊能力？
USED_ON  ⚠️
What season / time?  什么季节使用？（最易漏）
USED_WITH
What does it pair with?  通常搭配什么？
Passing score: ≥10 / 15 relations covered ✅ before any Listing goes live.  High-priority gaps: USED_FOR_EVE and USED_ON — most Chinese Listings score ❌ on both.  及格线：15 项中至少 10 项覆盖到位，低于及格线不允许上架。

PART 3 · OUR WORKFLOW

How We Work Now: 3 Mandatory Steps

我们现在的工作方式：每个新品上架前必须完成的三步

01Rufus 15-Question Audit  Rufus 15 问审计
Every Listing must pass a 15-relation COSMO coverage check before going live. Min score: 10/15 ✅. Failed Listings are revised, not shipped.  /  每个 Listing 上架前必须通过 15 关系覆盖检测，及格线 10/15，不及格不允许上架。
02Q&A as Priority 1  Q&A 是最高优先级
Q&A carries the highest Rufus weighting (UGC = Ground Truth to Rufus). We publish 15+ Q&As covering 10+ COSMO relations before launch.  /  Q&A 的 Rufus 权重最高，我们在上架前发布 15 条以上 Q&A，覆盖 10 种以上 COSMO 关系。
03Review Language Backfill  评论语言回灌
We mine competitor reviews, extract buyer language by COSMO relation type, and backfill into bullets and Q&A — because Rufus trusts UGC more than brand copy.  /  挖掘竞品评论语言，按关系类型回灌到五点和 Q&A，因为 Rufus 对 UGC 的信任度高于品牌自述。
PART 3 · SKILL IN DETAIL

The 10-Step Skill: How It Actually Runs

Rufus GEO Optimizer Skill — 渐进式加载，每步读一个参考文件、执行一件事，不跳步

Step 01–02  ·  前置必做
01  产品知识收集 — 调用 LinkFox API 拉取 Listing 数据 + 竞品评论
02  Rufus 15问模拟 — 对照 COSMO 关系逐一打分，找出 ✅/⚠️/❌，及格线 10/15
Step 03–06  ·  文案重构
03  关键词策略 — 传统 A9 + Rufus 语义关键词双层
04  标题撰写 — 150–200字符，前80字符含场景词，覆盖 ≥3 种关系
05  五点描述 — 场景痛点→机制→技术支撑，每条承载 2–3 种关系
06  A+ 内容 — alt-text 全填（Rufus 官方确认会读取图片标签）
Step 07  ·  Q&A 工程（权重最高）
生成 ≥15 条 Q&A，覆盖 ≥10 种 COSMO 关系。
Rufus 把 Q&A 当作 Ground Truth，信任度高于品牌自述。
六维评分中 Q&A 占 25分，权重最高。
Step 08–10  ·  收尾验证
08  后台属性 — Occasion / Season / Target Audience 等字段必须与文案 100% 一致
09  六维评分 — 100分制，低于75分不上架
10  输出报告 — COSMO矩阵 + 评分明细 + 可直接用的改写文案
Six Dimensions: Title 15  ·  Bullets 20  ·  A+ 20  ·  Q&A 25  ·  Data Consistency 10  ·  Image alt-text 10  标题15 + 五点20 + A+20 + Q&A 25 + 数据一致性10 + 图片alt-text10 = 100分
